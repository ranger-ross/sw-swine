About

S&W Swine Productions is located near Banquete, Texas which is 23 miles west of Corpus Christi

We offer a variety of breeds including Berkshire, Hampshire, Crosses, Yorkshire, Landrace, Duroc, Chester White and Spots. Purebred registration papers and Texas bred papers are available for exhibitors of the major stock shows.

Most of our farrowing occurs in mid-summer for the local county shows and the major stock shows. S&W Swine typically breeds between 35-55 sows yearly.

The farm is run by the family with occasional help from friends and family . We take pride in our animals and facilities and work hard to keep our animals healthy and happy. It is a tireless job.
